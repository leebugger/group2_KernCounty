Healthcare Services

Using California’s licensed‑facility dataset, this interactive map shows most clinic, hospital, and specialty center in Kern County. Pan and zoom to any area and and click a marker to see the facility’s name and if the facility is open or not.

This scatterplot compares California counties by percentage of uninsured residents and average number of mentally unhealthy days. Kern County, marked in red, falls above the state average in both metrics thus highlighting a potential link between limited insurance coverage and mental health challenges.

This bar graph breaks down the types of licensed healthcare facilities in Kern County. While general acute care hospitals and home health agencies are the majority, there is a noticeable shortage of specialized care facilities like psychiatric hospitals and rehabilitation clinics. This showcases key gaps in the mental and long-term health service infrastructure within Kern County and where they can improve next.
